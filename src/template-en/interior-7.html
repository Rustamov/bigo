Country house on the Kievsky motorway

550 m2 2018 New Moscow Photo Author Mikhail Stepanov

We were asked to refresh an existing interior in the shortest possible time of just six months. Upon closer examination, it turned out that many elements of the interior and decor needed replacing due to their poor quality, necessitating a rather large-scale reconstruction.

First of all, we cleared the double-tiered living room from any free-standing cupboards and shelves to open up the stunning panoramic view of the grounds. The ceiling was painted a dark colour and the ventilation integrated into the beams. The fireplace was replaced, and the specially constructed metal staircase became the pièce de résistance of the entire ground floor composition.
