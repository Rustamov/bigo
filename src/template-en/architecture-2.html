Landscape architecture and design in New Moscow

0.4 ha 2018 New Moscow Photo Author Ivan Boyko

For this project, we worked on designing the entire landscape with complex terraced terrain. We had to create a comfortable environment for receiving guests and a children's play area. To make the most of the useable space we came up with covered terraces and open areas; constructed a house with a spa, a garage and a roofed barbecue area.

We erected a children's playhouse on stilts above a steep slope, thus connecting two different levels of the site like a bridge. The space created underneath was perfect for a playground with a hammock, rope swing and climbing nets.

This spatial composition was finished with raw polished concrete and wood. Every architectural element of the landscape and all supplementary constructions were designed by us.
