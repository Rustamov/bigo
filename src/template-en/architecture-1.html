Guest house with spa

180 m2 2017 New Moscow Photo Author Dima Yagovkin

For receiving guests and enjoying the spa and hammam we designed this contemporary frame house. It was quick and easy to assemble, with internal wood finish, which turned out to be optimal both in terms of style and budget.

Two walls of the house are fully glazed and retractable, the roof morphs into the rear wall, which we decided to emphasize by finishing it with horizontal boarding just like the terrace. An outdoor spa pool is built into the terrace, with constantly circulating heated water it can be used all year round.
