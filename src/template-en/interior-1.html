Family House in Barcelona

370 m2 2019 Barcelona Photo Author Sergey Krasyuk

This four-storey house was purchased with a completed floor plan and interior. We carried out a large-scale reconstruction, changing the interior entirely, using every inch of space to maximum efficiency. For example, on the ground floor, we introduced a double-sided storage unit, which also serves as a partition between the living room and lobby.

The ground floor houses a communal area with living room, dining room and kitchen. The children's bedrooms with bathrooms and dressing rooms are on the first floor. The master bedroom and two study rooms are located on the second floor. As our clients requested the interior is in a calm modern Scandinavian style, which we combined with bright vintage objects of different eras from Spain and France.
