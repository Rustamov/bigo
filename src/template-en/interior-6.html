Country house with a medieval portal

500 m2 2016 New Moscow Photo Author Zinur Razutdinov

We had two starting points for this project: a finished house in the style of Frank Lloyd Wright and some antique sixteenth-century French chairs. The clients had their hearts set on a classic interior. This inspired us to create a museum-type space, combining antique and ultra-contemporary elements. We wanted to fabricate a story where the objects gathered under one roof from different eras would create the impression that the house has been lived in by several generations.

Itâ€™s all about the details in this interior, we got almost everything custom-made to our design, from doors and moulding to bronze hooks. The centrepiece is a Gothic style stucco portal in the foyer, which conceals the supporting columns.
