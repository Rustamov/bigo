Country house in New Moscow

650 m2 2019 New Moscow Photo Author Sergey Krasyuk

We worked together with interior decorator Elina Popova on this project in suburban Moscow. We completely redesigned the original facades of the house, built two extensions for children's rooms and changed the original floor plan for a classic symmetrical layout. The central axis is emphasized by a double-sided fireplace with a glass portal that opens both onto the hall and the dining room. The kitchen, dining and living rooms are located on the lateral axis, interconnected by large portals.

The interior features an amalgamation of different styles: some classical, some contemporary elements and lots of furniture, both in-built and freestanding, manufactured according to our design.
